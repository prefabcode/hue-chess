Hue Chess Settings

Reset Progress

Clear all your current progress and restart from level 1. This action will not clear your prestige level, it will only return you to level 1. To clear all data, please reinstall the Hue Chess extension.

Sync UI

Synchronize Lichess board settings with the Hue Chess profile stored on this computer. Use this if your current lichess board and color are desynchronized from your Hue Chess profile on this computer.
